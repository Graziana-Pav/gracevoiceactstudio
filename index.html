The essential is invisible to the eye

Grasping the essence is the purpose of our work

Graziana Pavone is a talented voice actor, influencer, and entrepreneur who has made her mark in the world of dubbing. With years of experience in animation, audiobooks, and commercials, she turned her passion for voice artistry into a mission to train the next generation of talent. This led to the creation of the Grace Dubbing Studio, a professional training hub for aspiring voice actors. Known for her dynamic teaching approach and industry expertise, Graziana inspires her students and followers alike, sharing behind-the-scenes insights and practical tips through her vibrant online presence.
